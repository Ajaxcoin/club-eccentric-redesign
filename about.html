About Club Eccentric

Club Eccentric is centrally located in the town centre of Southport, surrounded by all hotels and attractions in the area. Eccentric is a hotspot among gentlemen's clubs, offering upscale, discreet adult entertainment until the very late hours in an intimate, sumptuous interior and surrounding that will certainly enlighten your night out while you are visiting Southport or around the area in Merseyside.

Eccentric caters for everyone from discerning gentlemen to the high city roller while you're visiting the town for business or private reasons or simply treating yourself to an evening of sensual entertainment or planning a golf trip, corporate function, or special party. We offer a superb range of champagnes, wines, beers and spirits for you to choose from, Thursday through Sunday.

So settle yourself into one of our exquisite bars and spend some time with our stunning performers with continuous pole dancing shows all night long for your enjoyment.

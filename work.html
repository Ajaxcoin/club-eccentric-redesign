Dance For Us

Want to be a dancer? Come work for us at Southport's premier gentlemen's club.

Club Eccentric is always looking for confident, ambitious women to join our team of performers. Whether you're experienced or completely new to dancing, we'd love to hear from you.

Fantastic Earnings

Excellent earning potential with a supportive, professional environment.

Flexible Hours

Work hours that fit around your schedule. Open Thursday through Sunday.

Full Training

No experience necessary. Full training will be provided to all new dancers.

Requirements

  • Must be over the age of 18
  • Valid passport or relevant work permit
  • UK identification with current proof of address
  • No experience necessary. Training will be given

How to Apply

You are welcome to audition at Club Eccentric in person. Simply visit us during opening hours, or get in touch beforehand to arrange a time.
